create art for Gradient Descent

we are looking for visual arts that revel in the intensity of the future-now. cyberpunk as an aesthetic style is bold and psychadelic, playing with the intersection of human and machine. some iconic cyberpunk art comes from josan gonzalez and syd mead, and for more psychadelic work see kelly knowles, but these are not exhaustive. you are encouraged to experiment, to try new ideas, to do something unusual. if you would like to create an artwork to accompany a specific story in Gradient Descent, contact us and we will help you coordinate with an interested author.

interested contributors should consider the following guidelines:

  • independent art and work made to accompany a written piece are both encouraged
  • all images should be 300dpi and in .jpg, .gif or .png format
  • all mediums will be considered
  • please include a title for the piece, and a name (does not have to be your real name but can be)
  • send your piece as an attachment to graddesc@protonmail.com with the subject line "Art Submission" so we know what it is

details will be forthcoming regarding submissions, editing and promotion. this site will change and grow to reflect the state of the project. for suggestions/inquiries, contact graddesc@protonmail.com or contact the creator on twitter @HopefulAbandon
