write for Gradient Descent

we are looking for fiction pieces that reflect the interplay of technology and humanity, and that explore the implications of developments of the present and the near future, whether socially, philosophically or even theologically. cyberpunk fiction can be anything from ubik to god shaped hole, but these are by no means exhaustive. you are encouraged to experiment, to try new ideas, to do something unusual. of course, if your work is coherent, readable and of a reasonable length, it is more likely to become part of Gradient Descent. if there are problems with your piece we may ask you to revise or rewrite before rejecting, to make sure we don't throw out a good idea with a rought implementation.

interested contributors should consider the following guidelines:

  • fiction and nonfiction encouraged
  • written works should be in the range of 2k-5k words, although this is flexible
  • no porn, smut, obvious self-inserts or using other people's ip (fanfiction)
  • no plagiarism - should go without saying, we will check
  • please include a title for the piece, and a name (does not have to be your real name but can be)

details will be forthcoming regarding submissions, editing and promotion. this site will change and grow to reflect the state of the project. for suggestions/inquiries, contact graddesc@protonmail.com or contact the creator on twitter @HopefulAbandon
